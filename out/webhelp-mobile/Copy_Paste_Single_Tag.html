Copying and pasting a single tag

Copying and pasting a tag allow you to duplicate structure and/or content with minimal effort. This acts as a type of shortcut tor producing content in you document.

You can copy any single tag of your document, and paste it where ever you need to replicate that tag and/or information that tag contains.

  1. To copy and paste one tag (and its content in the document) click on the tag (until the tag is bolded but not italicized) and use the keyboard shortcut to copy (Ctrl C / Cmd C).

  2. Then select another tag or insert (using the right click menu) a tag in the structure panel, bolding the tag.

  3. Then use Ctrl V or Cmd V to paste the single tag in.
