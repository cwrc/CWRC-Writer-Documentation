Creating a date entity

The "Tag Date" is for tagging a date in the text. This tag can contain the year, month, day or a combination of the three.

  1. Highlight the text that you would like to tag as a "Date." Click on the toolbar or right-click options (see images below).
  2. Enter the date or the date range into the pop-up, and any additional available information.
You have added a date entity tag to the document.
