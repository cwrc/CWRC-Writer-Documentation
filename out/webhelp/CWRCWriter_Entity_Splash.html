Tagging RDF entities in CWRC-Writer

Introduction to tagging entities

Entities allow you to add semantic information to certain text. You can add entities to Person, Place, Date, Organization, Event, Citation, Note, Text/Title, Correction, Keyword, and Link. you can add qualifying information to the entities like the attributes precision and identification.
